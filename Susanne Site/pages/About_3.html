My Life in Square Dancing

I started dancing in September 2016. I started my calling career in July 2017. I am currently performing as a guest caller at various dances from Olympia to Stanwood. I called at the new caller's dance at the 2019 Callerlab Convention in Richmond, VA and the 2019 Washington State Festival in Puyallup, WA. I am a graduate of the 2019 Cascade Caller's College, the Gay Callers Association Callers' School, and the Women in Calling Institute. I was selected as Callerlab's 2019 "Top Dollar Caller".
